Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hive really free?

Yes. The desktop app is fully open source under MIT license. Every feature works with your own API keys at no cost. Cloud services (relay, AI gateway, sync) are optional paid add-ons.

Can I self-host the cloud services?

The desktop app is designed to work standalone. The cloud server is a separate product. You can always use your own API keys and local AI models without any cloud dependency.

What AI models are supported?

GPT-4o, GPT-4o-mini, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Claude 3 Haiku, Gemini Pro, Gemini Flash, LLaMA 3, Mixtral, and more. Plus local models via Ollama and LM Studio.

How does the AI gateway pricing work?

Pro includes $50/month in AI tokens routed through our gateway. Team includes $200/month. You're billed at a flat 1.3x of the provider's cost. Usage beyond budget requires your own API keys.

Is my data private?

Yes. Cloud sync uses client-side encryption (AES-256-GCM). We store opaque encrypted blobs — we can't read your data. Hive Shield scans for PII before anything leaves your machine.

What about skills and integrations?

Skills and integrations are always free for everyone. They're community-driven and anyone can create them. We believe extensibility should never be paywalled.
